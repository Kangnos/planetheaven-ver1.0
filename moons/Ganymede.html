What is Ganymede?

Ganymede is a satelite of Jupiter(JupiterIII), is the largest and most massive of the Solar System.
The ninth largest object in the Solar system, it is the largest without a substantial atmosphere.
It has a diameter of 5,28 km ( 3,273 mi) and is 8% larger than the planet Mercury,although only 45% as massive.
Possessing a metallic core, it has the lowest moment of inertia factor of any solid body
in the Solar System and is the only moon known to have a magnetic field. Ganymede orbits Jupiter in roughly seven days and is in a 1:2:4 orbital resonance with the moons Europa and Io, respectively.
