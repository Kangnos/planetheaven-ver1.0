What is Mimas?

Mimas, also designated Saturn I, is a moon of Saturn which was discovered in
1789 by William Herschel. It is named after Mimas, a son of Gaia in Greek mythology.
With a diameter of 396 killometers it is the smallest astronomical body that is knwon to
still be rounded in shape beacause of self-gravitation.
