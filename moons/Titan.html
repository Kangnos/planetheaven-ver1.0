What is Titan?

Titan is the largest moon of Saturn and the second-largest natural satelite in the
Solar System. it is the only moon known to have a dense atomosphere, and the only known body in space,
other than Earth, where clear evidence of stable bodies of surface liquid has been found.

Pictured in 2012 in natural color.
The thick atmosphere is orange due to a dense organonitrogen haze
