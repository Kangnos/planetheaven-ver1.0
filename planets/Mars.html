What is Mars?

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System
after Mercury. In English, Mars carries a name of the Roman god of war and is often referred
to as the 'Red Planet'. The latter refers to the ffect of the iron oxide prevalent on Mars's surface,
which gives it a reddish appearance distinctive among the astronomicval bodies visible to the naked eye.
