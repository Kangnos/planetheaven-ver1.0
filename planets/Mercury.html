What is Mercury?

Mercury is the smallest and innermost planet in the Solar system.
Its orbit around the Sun takes 87.92 days, the shortest of all the planets in the Solar System.
It is named after the Roamn deity Mercury, the messenger of the gods.
Mercury rotates in a way that is unique in the SolarSystem.
Itis tidally locked with the Sun in a 3:2 spin-orbit resonance.
