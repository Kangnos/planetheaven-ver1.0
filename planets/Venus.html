What is Venus?

Venus is the second palnet from the Sun. It is named after
the Roman goddes of love and beauty.
As the second-brightest natural object in the night sky after the Moon, Venus can
cast shadows and, rarely, is visible to the naked eye in broad day light.
Venus orbits the Sun every 224.7 Earth days. Venuse is a terrestal planet and is sometimes called Earth's "sister planet".
